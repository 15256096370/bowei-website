COMPANY OVERVIEW

A Specialized Production & Technical Service Company

Anhui Bowei Industrial Technology Co., Ltd. is located at Room 102, Bldg 12, Phase II, Liandong U Valley, Fanhua Avenue, Baohe District, Hefei. It is a specialized production & technical service company and a high-tech enterprise.

Our core management and technical staff each bring around 20 years of industry experience with rich expertise in design, process engineering and production. The company is equipped with imported OKK turning centers, Hardinge machining centers, 4.5-axis rotary tables, precision grinders, wire-cut EDM and other high-precision CNC equipment, plus high-precision CMM inspection instruments.

We specialize in process development, hydraulic workholding fixtures, non-standard automation equipment & integration, positioning and exchange systems — serving automotive parts, construction machinery, aerospace, medical and electronics industries.
